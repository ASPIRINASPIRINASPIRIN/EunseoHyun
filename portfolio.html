FRAGMENTS OF OBSCURITY [Video]

Self-initiated project which is based on the drawings of the old memories. "The idea for this project starts from a school where I had spent ten years of my childhood.
The memories of what I've been through at this school are quite old, faded, and even fragmented.
So when I looked at the diary which written 10 years ago, it was full of episodes
that I couldn't remember at all, so felt like it is not that realistic like a dream.
Then I put together these fragmented memories, which are as if I had only experienced in vision."
